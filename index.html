Privacy Policy for Shruti AI Tools

Last updated: February 2026

This application integrates with Google Workspace APIs (Gmail, Drive, Calendar, Contacts) to provide an AI assistant for personal productivity and organizational tasks at Yoga Research Foundation.

Data Use: We access email, drive files, calendar events, and contacts only to perform tasks explicitly requested by the account owner. No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

Data Storage: OAuth tokens are stored locally on the device in the operating system keychain. No data is stored on external servers.
